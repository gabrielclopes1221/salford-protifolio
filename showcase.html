Project Showcase

A selection of academic and commercial projects demonstrating my technical abilities.

Project 1: "The Local Bistro" Digital Transformation

Role: CTO / Lead Developer

Problem: The restaurant relied on phone bookings, leading to lost customers and operational inefficiency.

Design Concept & Rationale: For the "Case Study" page (see tab), I designed a centralized information hub. I prioritized the Contact Details at the top for immediate visibility. I implemented a JavaScript-powered form to automate booking requests via email, removing the need for a complex database while solving the manual phone issue. The layout is clean and responsive to appeal to the student demographic.
